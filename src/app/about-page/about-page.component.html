About

Battle Maps began as the senior capstone project of five computer science students, created throughout the fall of 2020 and spring of 2021. Given the project's limited time-frame, every effort was taken to include all the features players may find most useful.

Though that initial development is over, Battle Maps will continue to be maintained! If you come across any bugs or features you'd like to see implemented, send us an email using the “contact” link at the bottom of the page with the details of your bug or feature suggestion.
